Title: Grey Fox
Subtitle: Most basal, or “primitive” canine, Sympatric populations to Red fox and coyote
Weight: range 4-16lbs
Height: >1.5ft at withers
Physical attributes: Life expectancy ~4yr in the wild, 12 in captivity, Flexible tarsal bones provide a unique adaptation for climbing trees,Compact body, with petite, angular features, no white patterning, consistent “grizzling” coloration across the torso, Black dorsal stripe cross lower spine and tail, Experiencing generalized population decline to to habitat loss and competition with other mesopredators like coyote, Racoon, and weasel
