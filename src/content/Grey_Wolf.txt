Title: Grey Wolves
Subtitle: Largest of Canidae family
Weight: About 55-120lbs
Height: About 2.5 – 3ft
Length: About 5.5-6.5ft
Color:  Predominantly Agouti, Tawny, Black, or Silver
Physical attributes: Deep, V-shaped and narrow chest, long forelimbs, elbows extend below the chest, long, level back with tail carriage that hands low-to-level with the back, wide face with pronounced jaws, small and square ears in comparison to the face
