Title: Coyotes
Weight: 18lb-45lb depending on region
Height: Over 3ft 
Length: About 3.5ft
Color: Varies from a dusty sand to pseudo-melanistic, though rarely ever true black
Physical attributes: Deep chest, Compact body with narrow facial features and large, triangular ears “Cat Footed” with long middle toes and exposed nail
